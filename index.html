Learn more about Waynesville

Waynesville is located on scenic Route 66 in Missouri. It sits at the foot of the Ozarks mountains surrounded by rivers and streams, and is just a few miles west of Fort Leonard Wood.

When entering Waynesville you will be greeted by this handsome fellow, W. H. Croaky. We call him Froggy. Carved into the rock on the side a steep hill by local tattoo artist and former Army medic Phil Nelson. He was also known as a talented sculptor.

STAY FIT AND HEALTHY

Get your daily exercise and enjoy the scenery too! Beautiful parks, loads of walking trails, bike paths and a skate park too!

TRY A NEW ADVENTURE

Visit the the Ruobidoux Spring where the water is as clear as raindrops. Explore the underground cave, or try snorkeling in the underground river.

GREAT FUN FOR FAMILIES

Big Surf Water Park is a great place for families. Sip on a cool drink by the pool on a hot summer day, or splash with the kids in the kiddie pool.
